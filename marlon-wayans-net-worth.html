### Marlon Wayans Wiki Biography

Marlon L. Wayans is a well-known American comedian, director, model, writer, producer, and actor. His net worth is estimated to be $40 million.

Marlon, born on July 23, 1972, is part of the famous Wayans family known for their significant contribution to the American comedy scene. Hailing from New York, he was born to a housewife who was a part-time social worker and a supermarket manager. He is the youngest of his nine siblings, all involved in the entertainment industry.
