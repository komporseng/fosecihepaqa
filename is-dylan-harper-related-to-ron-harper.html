Dylan Harper's Family Tree

Dylan Harper, a five-star recruit in the Class of 2024, hails from a family deeply rooted in basketball.

He is the son of the accomplished 15-year NBA veteran, Ron Harper, and the younger brother of Ron Harper Jr., who has made a name for himself in the basketball world by playing at Rutgers and signing a two-way contract with the Toronto Raptors after excelling at the collegiate level.

Ron Harper: A Basketball Veteran

Ron Harper, the patriarch of the Harper family, enjoyed a successful career in the NBA, winning five championships and playing alongside the legendary Michael Jordan during his tenure with the Chicago Bulls.

His style of play and gusto for the game consider Harper as one of the best two-way players the league has ever scene.

Despite not attending Rutgers himself, Ron Harper’s influence and expertise in the sport have undoubtedly shaped the basketball journey of his sons, Ron Jr. and Dylan.

Ron Harper Jr.: Following in His Father's Footsteps

Ron Harper Jr., the elder brother of Dylan, has carved out his own path in the basketball world, mirroring his father's success to a certain extent.

As a four-year starter at Rutgers and a player with the Toronto Raptors, Ron Jr. has set a precedent for his younger brother, Dylan, as they both aim to leave their mark in the basketball arena.

Maria Harper: A Supportive Matriarch

Maria Harper, the mother of Dylan and Ron Jr., is an integral figure in their lives. She currently serves as an assistant coach at Don Bosco Prep, the institution where Dylan is honing his skills. 

The Influence of Ron Harper on Dylan's Career

The presence of Ron Harper, a seasoned NBA player, in Dylan's life has undoubtedly impacted his approach to the game.
