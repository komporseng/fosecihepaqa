Who Is Jonbers Blonde? Her Partner, Real Name

Publish date: 2024-09-06

Meet Jonbers Blonde from RuPaul’s Drag Race UK season 4. Learn about her partner, her real name, and her job. The new season just premiered in mid-September 2022 and the line-up looks stylish, confident, and powerful to say the least.

Jonbers Blonde On RuPaul’s Drag Race UK

Jonbers Blond might be new on RuPaul’s Drag Race UK but she has been doing drag for 14 years. She described herself as a “showgirl on a Saturday and an absolute bin-fire on a Sunday”. The self-described “high-fashion pink” has performed with artists like Melanie C, Nadine Coyle, and Sam Smith, all over the world and has worked as a background dancer for artists like Years and Years and Jessie Ware.

When asked where the Drag name came from, Jonbers said: “One night, I was at my friend’s house dressed to the nines and I came down the stairs and my friend’s dad looked up from his paper and said, ‘Jonbers, looking like that you’re going to get very lucky!’ and since then “Jonbers” just stuck!”

According to Jonbers, fans can anticipate “a lot of movement” from him, adding that it’s “like coming to watch a crab perform.”

Jonbers said: “There’s a lot of moving back and forth. I‘m a storyteller too, so accompanied by my Irish flute, you‘ll get a great yarn with plenty of animal and Irish references.” Jonbers who has been doing drag for over a decade but “good drag for the last six (years)”, reckons she can take home the crown thanks to her plan to “go big and wild”.

In addition to Jonbers, you’ll also meet Danny Beard, Baby, Cheddar Gorgeous, Just May, Starlet, Black Peppa, Coppa Top Queen, Dakota Schiffer, Sminty Drop, Le Fil, and Pixie Polite.

She believes she is the whole package and is qualified to wind the show. She said, “I’ve got the back catalog. I can dance. I can move. I’ve got fashion skills. I’m not afraid to be silly. I’ve got a unique voice. You put all that together and you got this big muppet here! What more do you want?”

“I really do have the fashion skills, as I’m also a stylist. I have worked at Vogue International and I have been the fashion editor of several magazines. I’ve also walked the runways in London and Paris,” Jonbers stated. “I’m going to treat each runway as RuPaul’s Met Ball, Each week, I plan to go big and wild! Although I am a high fashion girl, I’m not afraid to get mucky. I’m like a pig in muck!”

Jonbers Blonde Partner

Jonbers Blonde loves to keep her romantic life under wraps. She never shared any details regarding who is her partner in 2022 at the time of the premiere of the show. In addition, her social media posts didn’t confirm who she is dating currently. So, we would like to conclude that she is single as of the premiere of the show or maintaining a relationship away from the limelight.

In July 2022, she posted about attending a queer wedding. She congratulated the married couple and wrote, “To two of the greatest people I know, that was my first queer wedding and hopefully not my last. We cried, we danced and I took some dodgy photos.”

What Is Jonbers Blonde Real Name?

Jonbers Blonde also goes by the name Jonbenet Blonde. Her real name is Andrew Glover (@jonbenet.blonde).

How Old Is Jonbers Blonde?

Jonbers Blonde Height

Jonbers Blonde Job

Jonbers Blonde works as a DJ, performer, stylist, and event producer. She is the elusive queen of East London drag. To the Catwalk From Cattle Run Over ten years ago, this Northern Irish queen burst into the London scene. One of Sink the Pink’s creative directors for the past five years is The Original “Fash Mag Slag.” Additionally, a DJ who has performed at weddings and birthday parties for STP, Agent Provocateur, YSL, MAC, Anabelles, Soho House Group, and numerous others.

She is the only Drag Queens to ever play on Glastonbury’s Pyramid Stage. Over 14 years, she has worked on show production, DJing, and live performance in London. She also worked as the creative director of Sink The Pink and shared the stage alongside pop icons including Little Mix, Mabel, Melanie C, Arcade Fire, Jessie Ware, The Pussycat Dolls, Zara Larsson, Nadine Coyle, Sophie Ellis Bextor, and many more.

You can also find her on YouTube. In early 2022, she also became Britney Spears for Attitude Magazine.
