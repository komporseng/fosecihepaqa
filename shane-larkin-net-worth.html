Shane Larkin: From Miami Star to NBA Player

Shane Larkin is a professional basketball player with a net worth of $5 million. He was born in Cincinnati, Ohio in 1992 and played basketball at Dr. Phillips High School before attending the University of Miami.

Larkin was a standout player at Miami, where he was named ACC Player of the Year by the coaches in 2013 and won the Lute Olson Award. He was also a second-team All-American by AP and NABC and a third-team All-American by SN that same year. He made first-team All-ACC in 2013 after making the ACC All-Freshman team in 2012.

Larkin was drafted 18th overall by the Atlanta Hawks in the 2013 NBA Draft and was later traded to the Dallas Mavericks, where he played from 2013 to 2014. He then played for the New York Knicks from 2014 to 2015 and the Brooklyn Nets from 2015 to 2016 before heading to Spain to play for Baskonia. While there, he was named to the All-Spanish League Second Team.

In 2017, Larkin joined the Boston Celtics, where he currently plays. He is the son of Baseball Hall of Famer Barry Larkin.
