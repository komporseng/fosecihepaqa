Biography

Aaron Kwok was born in Hong Kong, China on October 26, 1965. Chinese pop vocalist and dancer known for his Michael Jackson-influenced style of dancing. He won a Top Ten Hong Kong Dance Award and an Asia Pacific Most Popular Artist Award. Janet Jackson collaborated with him on the song “Ask For More,” which was used for a Pepsi campaign. After graduating from St. Joseph’s College, he worked at the King Fook Gold & Jewellery Company.
