Biography

Lee Brice was born in Sumter, SC on June 10, 1979. Country singer who released the platinum-selling singles “Love Like Crazy,” “A Woman Like You,” and “Hard to Love.” His song “I Don’t Dance” was named Single Record of the Year at the 2015 ACM Awards. He has written songs for many successful country singers, including Garth Brooks. Brice learned how to play piano and also sang in his church. He earned a scholarship to play football at Clemson University, but his career was ended by an arm injury.
